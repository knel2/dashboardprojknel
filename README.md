## Explanation of Map
### Since the project gave two optionsof maps to chose between I decided to chose to do proprotional map to look at the wildfires in Washington state to look at the magnitude of the number of acres burned in names of wilfire cases that are captured from 2020-2026 data. It also shows the impact of the wildfires clearly by dividi For the  [Dataset used from geo.wa.gov Fire Statistics 2008-present](https://geo.wa.gov/datasets/wadnr::dnr-fire-statistics-2008-present/explore?location=48.109870%2C-121.027991%2C9). Although in the title of dataset it says that the data is from 2008-present I filtered it to be from 2020-2026 to gather information from the last 5 years to make the dataset more condensed and to see the most up to date data in washington state
